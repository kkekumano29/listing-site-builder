About this home

Live above it all in this well-situated 2-bedroom, 2-bath condo in the heart of Honolulu. Perched on the 10th floor of Hale O Pumehana, the home captures sweeping city, ocean, and sunset views from an efficient 786-square-foot layout. Minutes from Downtown, Ward Village, and Kaka'ako, you're at the center of everything the island has to offer — dining, shopping, the waterfront, and quick freeway access. With VA, conventional, and cash financing all accepted, this is a rare opportunity for buyers, veterans, and investors to own in one of O'ahu's most sought-after central corridors. Central AC, in-unit laundry, and a covered parking stall complete the package.

The neighborhood

Downtown & Kaka'ako

Steps from the energy of urban Honolulu — walkable to restaurants, coffee shops, boutiques, and the waterfront. Ward Village and Kaka'ako's art and dining scene are minutes away.

One of the most central locations on O'ahu. Quick on-ramp access to H-1 makes commuting to any part of the island fast and straightforward.
